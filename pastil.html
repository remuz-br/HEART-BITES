Puso ng Saging Pastil

Traditional Filipino rice dish with savory banana blossom filling wrapped in banana leaves

A guilt-free snack packed with fiber and nutrients

Ingredients

  • Puso ng saging (banana blossom), chopped
  • Oil
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Laurel leaf (bay leaf)
  • Black pepper
  • Turmeric powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Steamed rice
  • Banana leaves (optional, for wrapping)

Preparation Steps

  1. Peel and chop puso ng saging finely. Soak in water with salt or vinegar for 10 minutes. Rinse and drain well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame.
  3. Saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant and slightly golden.
  4. Add the drained puso ng saging and stir well.
  5. Season with laurel leaf, black pepper, and turmeric powder. Mix to coat evenly.
  6. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar. Do not stir immediately‚Äîlet it simmer for 2 minutes.
  7. Add sugar to balance the flavor. Stir well.
  8. Continue cooking for 5‚Äì10 minutes until tender and sauce is absorbed.
  9. Serve over steamed rice and wrap in banana leaves, just like traditional pastil!
