Puso ng Saging Siomai

Steamed banana blossom to perfection

A guilt-free snack packed with fiber and nutrients

Ingredients

  • 2 Banana blossoms (puso ng saging)
  • 1 tablespoon Knorr seasoning
  • 1 egg
  • Molo wrapper (siomai wrapper)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 2 Carrots, grated
  • 5 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pc Celery
  • 2 pc Onions, chopped
  • 1 pc Onion leaves
  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • Soy sauce and vinegar with ginger strips (for dipping)

Preparation Steps

  1. Remove tough outer layers of banana blossom. Chop tender inner part finely.
  2. Soak in water with lemon for 15‚Äì20 minutes to remove bitterness. Rinse and squeeze out excess water.
  3. In a bowl, combine chopped banana blossom, grated carrot, onion, and garlic.
  4. Add Knorr seasoning, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined and sticky.
  5. Place 1 tablespoon of mixture in the center of a siomai wrapper. Gather sides and press to form shape.
  6. Arrange in a steamer lined with parchment paper or banana leaf.
  7. Steam for 15‚Äì20 minutes until firm and cooked through.
  8. Serve warm with soy-vinegar dipping sauce and ginger strips!

Health Benefits

Why Choose Puso ng Saging:

  • Low-fat alternative to traditional pork siomai
  • High in dietary fiber - keeps you full longer
  • Plant-based protein source
  • Rich in potassium - supports healthy blood pressure
  • Contains essential minerals like calcium and phosphorus
  • Steamed preparation retains more nutrients
