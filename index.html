Crispy Balat ng Puso ng Saging

Crispy and golden fried banana blossom peels - a delicious vegetarian snack!

▼

QR Code

Ingredients

  • Balat ng puso ng saging
  • Lemon (for soaking)
  • Cornstarch
  • Chicken breading
  • Paprika
  • Egg
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil (for frying)

Sauce:

  • 2 garlic cloves, finely grated
  • 6 Tbsp. mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp. fresh lemon juice

Procedures

  1. Prepare the balat (peel) ng puso ng saging. Carefully remove the outer layers until you reach the soft inner layers. Slice into desired sizes.
  2. Soak in lemon water for 10–15 minutes to remove bitterness, then rinse and drain well.
  3. Season the balat with salt and black pepper.
  4. Prepare coating mixture by combining cornstarch, chicken breading, and paprika.
  5. Beat egg in a bowl, then dip each piece of balat into the egg mixture.
  6. Coat with breading mixture until fully covered.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  8. Fry until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  9. Mix sauce ingredients and serve hot!

Health Benefits

Puso ng Saging (Banana Blossom) Benefits:

  • Rich in fiber - aids digestion and promotes gut health
  • Low in calories - perfect for weight management
  • Contains vitamins A, C, and E - boosts immunity
  • High in antioxidants - fights free radicals
  • Helps regulate blood sugar levels
  • Contains iron and magnesium - supports heart health

Puso ng Saging Siomai

Healthy and flavorful steamed dumplings with banana blossom filling

▼

QR Code

QR Code Here

Ingredients

  • 2 Banana blossoms (puso ng saging)
  • 1 tablespoon Knorr seasoning
  • 1 egg
  • Molo wrapper (siomai wrapper)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 2 Carrots, grated
  • 5 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pc Celery
  • 2 pc Onions, chopped
  • 1 pc Onion leaves
  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • Soy sauce and vinegar with ginger strips (for dipping)

Procedures

  1. Remove tough outer layers of banana blossom. Chop tender inner part finely.
  2. Soak in water with lemon for 15–20 minutes to remove bitterness. Rinse and squeeze out excess water.
  3. In a bowl, combine chopped banana blossom, grated carrot, onion, and garlic.
  4. Add Knorr seasoning, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined and sticky.
  5. Place 1 tablespoon of mixture in the center of a siomai wrapper. Gather sides and press to form shape.
  6. Arrange in a steamer lined with parchment paper or banana leaf.
  7. Steam for 15–20 minutes until firm and cooked through.
  8. Serve warm with soy-vinegar dipping sauce and ginger strips!

Health Benefits

Why Choose Puso ng Saging Siomai:

  • Low-fat alternative to traditional pork siomai
  • High in dietary fiber - keeps you full longer
  • Plant-based protein source
  • Rich in potassium - supports healthy blood pressure
  • Contains essential minerals like calcium and phosphorus
  • Steamed preparation retains more nutrients

Puso ng Saging Pastil

Traditional Filipino rice dish with savory banana blossom filling wrapped in banana leaves

▼

QR Code Here

Ingredients

  • Puso ng saging (banana blossom), chopped
  • Oil
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Laurel leaf (bay leaf)
  • Black pepper
  • Turmeric powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Steamed rice
  • Banana leaves (optional, for wrapping)

Procedures

  1. Peel and chop puso ng saging finely. Soak in water with salt or vinegar for 10 minutes. Rinse and drain well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant and slightly golden.
  4. Add the drained puso ng saging and stir well.
  5. Season with laurel leaf, black pepper, and turmeric powder. Mix to coat evenly.
  6. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar. Do not stir immediately—let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  7. Add sugar to balance the flavor. Stir well.
  8. Continue cooking for 5–10 minutes until tender and sauce is absorbed.
  9. Serve over steamed rice and wrap in banana leaves, just like traditional pastil!

Health Benefits

Nutritional Advantages:

  • Turmeric adds anti-inflammatory properties
  • Complete vegetarian meal option
  • High in fiber - promotes digestive health
  • Low in cholesterol and saturated fats
  • Rich in antioxidants from banana blossom
  • Provides sustained energy from complex carbs
  • Traditional preparation preserves natural nutrients

Banana Blossom Smoothie

Refreshing and nutritious smoothie blend - a perfect healthy drink!

QR Code Here

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/4 cup puso ng saging (banana blossom), tender inner parts
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 2-3 tablespoons condensed milk (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Optional: 1 tsp honey or vanilla extract for extra flavor

Procedures

  1. Prepare the banana blossom by removing tough outer layers and extracting the tender inner portions.
  2. Soak banana blossom in water with a bit of lemon juice for 5 minutes to reduce any bitterness. Rinse and drain well.
  3. Peel the banana and break it into chunks.
  4. Add all ingredients to a blender: banana, prepared banana blossom, milk, condensed milk, and ice cubes.
  5. Blend on high speed for 1-2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  6. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more condensed milk or honey if desired.
  7. Pour into a glass and serve immediately. Enjoy your nutritious smoothie!

Tip: For a thicker smoothie, use frozen banana or add more ice!

Health Benefits

Nutrient-Packed Refreshment:

  • Excellent source of potassium from bananas
  • Rich in vitamins and minerals from banana blossom
  • Provides calcium and protein from milk
  • Natural energy boost - perfect for breakfast or post-workout
  • High in dietary fiber - promotes healthy digestion
  • Contains antioxidants for immune support
  • Creamy texture without artificial thickeners
  • Naturally sweet - minimal added sugar needed
