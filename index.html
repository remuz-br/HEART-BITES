About Heart Bites

Bringing the goodness of nature to your table, one bite at a time

Our Story

Heart Bites was born from a simple belief: healthy snacking should never compromise on taste. We discovered the incredible nutritional power of Puso ng Saging (banana blossom heart) and knew we had to share it with the world.

Each crispy bite is carefully crafted to preserve the natural goodness of this Filipino superfood. Rich in fiber, antioxidants, and essential minerals, our snacks prove that nature's bounty can be both delicious and nutritious.

We're committed to sustainable farming practices, supporting local communities, and bringing you snacks that are as good for the planet as they are for your body.
